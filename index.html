PARADISE BEACH RESORT

Facilities

Seaside Dormitory House

A stilted beachside house used as a dormitory or guest room. Its height protects from sand and water during high tide. Great for accommodating staff, tourists, or volunteers. The wooden veranda gives a relaxing view of the beach.

Coastal Lifeguard Post

A simple elevated watchtower located right on the beach, used by lifeguards or safety personnel to oversee swimmers and beachgoers. Offers a clear view of the shoreline for quick response during emergencies.

Beach Community Hall

A large, elevated native-style hall made from concrete bamboo design, ideal for hosting events, orientations, or large group activities. Its spacious design and beachside location make it a flexible venue for community events, guest receptions, or indoor beach programs.

Beachside Dining Spot

A durable, concrete picnic table set in an open area near the beach, perfect for group meals, casual dining, or social gatherings. Positioned under tree shade and near other amenities, this table setup offers a cozy and accessible space for visitors to enjoy food and drinks by the sea.
